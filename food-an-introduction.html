Food!: An Introduction

March 29, 2020

So when I tell people that I don’t want to be a software engineer for the long haul (however, I do enjoy working at my current job a lot right now!), then their natural follow-up is: what DO you want to do then?

And my answer is always: I would like to be a food entrepreneur. In what capacity I don’t know yet— but I can see myself being a cafe owner (Tatte, I’m coming for you), founder of a consumer packaged goods startup, designer for a snack brand, or venture capitalist for a food-focused fund.

However, I have little to show for why I should be an innovator in the food industry other than a failed attempt at commercializing an algae snack bar and a disastrous summer experience at a BBQ startup, both qualifying more as caricatures of my air-headed college self than actual work experience.

Hence I am writing this series, which I am calling "Food!”, that will cover my thoughts on the consumer food industry.

So yeah, you’re thinking “Food!"— what a laaaame name. But here were the other candidates (and my annotations), and you tell me if any of them are better. (They’re not.)

Alternate titles for this series:

  • Gastromonica— lol cringe, and I also don’t intend to talk about gastronomy
  • Food Pioneers— love this topic, but what if I want to talk about food dinosaurs?
  • DOOF— a nod to Erewhon, a super upscale Los Angeles-only grocer, but doesn’t have quite the same ring
  • The Barcode— a neat idea since this series will be primarily devoted to things that can be scanned and purchased at grocery stores, but this name would be better if I were solely analyzing the beverage industry
  • The Supermarket Stroll/ In My Basket/ The Checkout Line/ The Aisle Analysis— um, this is not a mommy blog.
  • The Zhuzh— fun word, that’s all
